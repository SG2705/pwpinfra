Raghav Tandon

Co-Founder and Partner

Raghav is a B.Tech Civil Engineer with specialization in Infrastructure Management from IITRAM, Ahmedabad, bringing deep expertise across infrastructure, industrial projects, and turnkey execution. He began his career at Tata Consulting Engineers as a Techno-Commercial Professional, gaining exposure to large-scale infrastructure planning and execution.

Driven by an early entrepreneurial vision, he transitioned into the startup ecosystem by joining Baseline Project Management Services, Vadodara, where he handled end-to-end project responsibilities including tendering, bid & contract management, site execution, engineering coordination, business development, and market research across industrial projects such as manufacturing plants, airports, and power plants.

He successfully led projects for marquee clients including Anand Rathi Financial Services, Mahindra Finance, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Asian Paints, Adani Enterprise, and MENA Global, while also contributing to internal operations and process management.

He became a Core member of SQER Projects Pvt. Ltd., scaling the company in its first year and also working as independent consultant for Project Management Assignments.

After gaining hands-on experience and confidence to run a business, he founded PwP Infra LLP to redefine turnkey execution by integrating market research, real estate advisory, and design-build delivery.
